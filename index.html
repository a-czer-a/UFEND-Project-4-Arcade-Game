The Classic Arcade Game

  • To win the game the player has to reach the water with at least 700 points.
  • To move the player use arrow keys.
  • The player has 3 lives and looses one after collision with a bug enemy.
  • The player gets 50 points every time he reaches the water.
  • The player can collect gems of a different value and earn extra points.
  • An extra life can be collected during a game.
